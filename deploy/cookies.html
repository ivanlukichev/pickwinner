Cookies Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files that a website can store in your browser to help remember preferences, improve performance, or understand how pages are being used.

How PickWinner Tools May Use Cookies

PickWinner Tools may use cookies or similar browser storage for a limited set of purposes, such as remembering interface preferences, supporting site performance, or measuring aggregate usage.

Analytics Cookies

If analytics tools are enabled, cookies may be used to understand page visits, traffic sources, general device information, and how visitors interact with the site. This data is intended for service improvement and not for creating user accounts.

Performance and Preference Storage

Some tools on the site also store settings directly in local browser storage so users can reload a tool without losing setup data. This client-side storage is separate from account-based tracking and is used only to improve the tool experience.

How to Disable Cookies

You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings. You can also clear local browser storage if you want to remove saved tool configurations. Disabling cookies may affect convenience features or analytics accuracy, but the site should remain usable for normal browsing.
