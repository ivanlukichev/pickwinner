    How to use Spin the Wheel for giveaways, classrooms, and quick picks

    This spin the wheel tool is built for quick, fair picks that work directly in the browser. Add names, prizes, prompts, or choices, then let the wheel reveal a winner with a smooth animation that reflects an honest client-side random selection.

    It works well for giveaway picks, classroom activities, party games, light team exercises, and fast decision-making moments where you want something more visual than a plain random list. Because the setup lives on the page, it stays simple to share, reset, and reuse across devices.

    FAQ

    Spin the Wheel FAQ

    Is this random wheel fair?

    Yes. The result is selected before the spin animation starts, so the animation is only a visual reveal of that fair outcome.

    Can I remove winning sections after each spin?

    Yes. You can remove winners or cap repeat wins depending on how you want the wheel to behave.

    Can I share my wheel setup?

    Yes. The share link keeps your current items and settings in a reusable URL.

    Does it work on mobile?

    Yes. The wheel interface and settings are built to work well on phones, tablets, and desktops.
